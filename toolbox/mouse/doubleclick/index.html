How to use this Mouse Test tool?

This tool tests all basic functions of your mouse. Click any button (Left, Right, Middle, Side buttons) to see them light up on the visualizer. The panel on the right tracks your click counts, double clicks, and faulty double clicks.

How to detect Mouse Double Clicking?

The "Double Click Bug" happens when a single physical press registers as two inputs due to switch degradation. The tool tracks three types of clicks:

  • Click: Any button press counts as one click
  • Double Click: Two clicks within 80-500ms interval (normal double click)
  • Faulty Double Click: Two clicks with interval less than 80ms (indicates hardware issue)

All Buttons Can Have Double Click Issues

Not just left button - right button, middle button, and side buttons can also develop double click faults over time. Check the counters for each button separately.

Common Troubleshooting

  • High Faulty Double Click Count: If faulty double click count increases when you only click once, your mouse switch might be failing.
  • Unresponsive Button: If a button does not light up, the switch may be broken.
  • Right-click displaying pressed state: In some browsers, if you press and hold the right mouse button while simultaneously moving the mouse, and then release the right mouse button, the right mouse button will still display as pressed. Therefore, it is best not to move the mouse when testing key presses.
