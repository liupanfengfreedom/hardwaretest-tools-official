What na APY (Annual Percentage Yield)?

APY reflects di real rate of return when compounding na taken into account. In daily compound mode, dis calculator automatically converts di nominal annual rate into APY, allowing you to see di true difference between compound and simple interest.

Why use a 360-day year instead of 365?

International financial markets (e.g., interbank rates, some deposit products) often use a 360-day convention for simplicity and consistency. Dis tool follows dat standard to align with institutional interest rate quotes.

How do I quickly compare current and fixed deposit returns?

Simply switch between "Daily compound (current)" and "Simple interest (fixed)" under Product Type. Typical rates (0.35% for current, 2.75% for fixed) na auto-filled, but you fit adjust them manually. Di chart updates instantly to show di growth path.

What na di interest on 100 million $ deposited for one year?

Use dis calculator: enter 100,000,000 as principal, choose 1 year, and select current or fixed. At typical rates, current (0.35%) yields about 35,000 $, fixed (2.75%) yields about 2,750,000 $. Actual rates depend on your bank; dis tool gives a quick comparison.
