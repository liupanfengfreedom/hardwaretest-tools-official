Wetin be password entropy and why e matter?

Password entropy measures randomness and unpredictability, expressed in bits. Higher entropy means stronger passwords dat na harder to crack. Formula: entropy = log₂(charset_size ^ password_length). For example, a 12-character password from a 94-character set yields ~78.5 bits of entropy.

Why person go remove some characters?

Excluding characters improves usability: 1) Avoid confusion (0/O, 1/l, 5/S). 2) Some systems restrict special symbols. 3) Easier input on mobile devices. However, note dat excluding characters reduces charset size and may slightly impact security.
