What na a QR code? What types exist?

A QR Code (Quick Response Code) na a 2D barcode dat stores more data than traditional 1D barcodes. Main types include:
1) Standard QR Code: Common black‑and‑white matrix
2) Micro QR Code: Smaller version for limited space
3) Design QR Code: Customized with logos, colors, and patterns
4) Dynamic QR Code: Updatable content (requires server support)

What sizes na recommended for different use cases?

Choose QR code size based on usage scenario:
Digital media: 150‑300px (websites, apps, social media)
Print materials: 300‑600px (business cards, flyers, posters)
Outdoor advertising: 600‑800px (large displays, vehicle ads)
Product packaging: 200‑400px (adjust based on package size)

Which color combinations affect QR code scanning?

Dese combinations may reduce scan success:
1) Light‑colored QR codes on light backgrounds
2) Red/orange combinations (some scanners na sensitive to warm colors)
3) Insufficient contrast between similar colors
4) Recommended: dark QR codes (black, dark blue, dark green) on light backgrounds
