Frequently Asked Questions

how to record MP3?
Currently, dis tool supports exporting to WEBM (original quality) and WAV (lossless). If you need MP3 format, we recommend exporting to WAV first and then using ouraudio converter toolto convert it. We plan to support direct MP3 export in future versions – stay tuned!
How do I record system audio?
Select "System audio" as source and click REC. In di browser share dialog, pick any share target (e.g. entire screen) and make sure to check "Share system audio" at di bottom. Confirm to start.
Why can't I capture system sound?
Most likely you forgot to check di "Share audio" checkbox in di popup. Also, macOS needs BlackHole installed; Firefox does not support system audio – use Chrome/Edge.
What options appear in di share dialog?
You'll see three tabs: "Chrome Tab", "Window", "Entire screen". Choose any, but you must check di "Share system audio" box at di bottom, then click Share.
Which export formats na available?
In settings you fit select auto‑export: WEBM (original) or WAV (lossless). Without auto‑export, recordings stay in di list for later manual export.
Use guide am am am am scheduled stop?
Fill a number in "Scheduled stop" and choose unit (sec/min/hour). E.g. 5 + minutes = stop after 5 minutes. 0 or empty = manual stop.
