Question wey people dey ask well well (FAQ)

Why na my camera not displaying?

Dis may be due to browser permission restrictions or camera hardware issues. Please ensure: 1) You allow di browser to access di camera; 2) Di camera na properly connected; 3) No other application na currently using di camera.

How do I test camera frame rate?

Look at di real-time FPS value in di top right corner of di page. Pro cameras typically support 30fps or higher, high-end cameras fit reach 60fps or more. A stable frame rate na key to smooth video.

How fit I tell if my camera quality na poor?

If you notice: 1) Blurry video; 2) Low frame rate causing stuttering; 3) Color distortion; 4) Poor performance in low light, dese may indicate camera quality issues.

What types of cameras na supported?

All types of cameras na supported: laptop built-in cameras, USB external cameras, webcams, conference cameras, live streaming cameras, etc. Compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux.

How do I test camera resolution?

The tool automatically displays di current resolution. High-quality cameras usually support 1080p (1920x1080) or higher, while standard cameras may only support 720p (1280x720).

Na my test data saved?

All test data na stored only in your current browser session. It go be lost when you refresh di page or close di tab. We do not collect or upload any camera data, ensuring your privacy.

Data security: all data processed locally
