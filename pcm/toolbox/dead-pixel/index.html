Question people dey ask well well (FAQ)

What na screen dead pixels?

Screen dead pixels na defective pixels on LCD displays dat cannot display colors properly. Mainly divided into bright spots (always lit pixels), dead pixels (always dark pixels), and stuck pixels (pixels showing wrong colors).

Na it normal for new monitors to have dead pixels?

Most manufacturers allow a certain number of dead pixels, with specific standards varying by manufacturer. Typically, dead pixels in central areas na more serious than edge areas. When purchasing new monitors, check di dead pixel policy.

Can dead pixels be fixed?

Physical dead pixels usually cannot be repaired, but sometimes "stuck" pixels fit be attempted through software methods. Dis tool does not provide repair functions, only detection.

Why test multiple colors?

Different types of dead pixels na easier to detect on different color backgrounds. For example: black background detects bright spots, white background detects dead pixels, solid colors detect stuck pixels.

What suppose I pay attention to during testing?

View from different angles as some dead pixels may only be visible from specific viewpoints. Also, distinguish between dust on screen surface and actual dead pixels.

What if test results na inaccurate?

If you suspect test results, use other testing tools or repeat tests in different environments. In some cases, graphics driver issues may also cause display abnormalities.
