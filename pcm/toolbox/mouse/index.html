Question people dey ask well well

How I fit test mouse side work?

Press any mouse side. Di corresponding 'Pressed' counter on di right go increase. When you release di side, di 'Released' counter also increases. If di counters don't change, di side fit get wahala. A normal side suppose respond to both press and release actions.

How I fit test mouse side sides well?

Press di mouse side sides, wey people usually label B4 and B5. Di matching "Press" and "Release" counters for di side sides suppose increase. If dem no move, di side sides fit get wahala. Side sides often dey for di left side of di mouse for forward/back work or custom functions.

Wetin "Press" and "Release" counts mean?

The 'Pressed' count tracks how many times you've pressed di side down. Di 'Released' count tracks how many times you've let go of di side. For normal mouse operation, dese two numbers suppose match when you release di side. A mismatch might indicate di side na sticking or not releasing properly. A large difference suggests a mechanical wahala.

Why na di counter showing number pass my real taps?

Dis most likely points to abnormal side contact, possibly caused by:

  • Micro-switch aging or oxidation causing poor contact
  • Dust accumulation inside di side
  • Physical damage causing frequent contact make/break

Why di scroll test separate 'Up' and 'Down' counts?

Separate counts for scroll up and down help diagnose specific wahala with di scroll wheel encoder. For example, if only 'Up' counts increase while 'Down' no answer, e fit point to poor contact or damage for one direction of di encoder.

Tip: Watch for bounce-back or stuttering when you test di scroll wheel Special wheel page

How I go know if my mouse needs replacing?

Consider replacing your mouse if you notice di following:

  • 1) Sides don't answer when pressed;
  • 2) Random double taps or multi taps dey happen, and a special double-tap page confirms say na mechanical wahala;
  • 3) Scroll wheel no dey steady or e dey scroll backward, and a special wheel page confirms say na mechanical wahala;
  • 4) Side sides don't work.

Dis page fit help you systematically diagnose dese wahala.
