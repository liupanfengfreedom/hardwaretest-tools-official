How to use di page-side double-tap page

Step-by-step way to test mouse sides, scroll wheel, side sides, and usual double-tap wahala.

📖

About dis tool

Dis page-side mouse check page helps you quickly know whether your mouse parts still dey work well, without installing any extra app. With visual answer and live data feedback, you fit understand the real condition of the mouse better.

Di page supports left side, right side, middle side, two side sides (B4/B5), scroll wheel up/down, and faulty double-tap wahala spotting.

1

Enter di test area

Move your mouse into di "Mouse check area". Once the mouse enter, di border go turn blue and the status go show say testing don activate.

Important note:Testing only works inside di test area. Make sure your mouse really dey inside di gray border box.
🖱️

Test area status signs

Border color changes dey show current test status:

  • Gray: Dey ready
  • Blue: Don active
  • Light Blue: Testing dey happen
  • Green: Double-tap don show
  • Red: Wahala don show
2

Check mouse sides

Inside di test area, tap each mouse side one by one:

🖱️

Left-side check

Tap di left mouse side and watch whether di left-side area lights up. The left-side count for that side suppose rise.

🖱️

Right-side check

Tap di right mouse side and confirm say di right-side area lights up. Di matching count for the right panel suppose rise too.

🖱️

Middle-side check

Press the mouse wheel as middle side. The middle area suppose light up and the middle-side count suppose increase.

Key thing to watch:Each side suppose give clean press and release feedback. For normal side, di "Press" and "Release" counts suppose match.
3

Check side sides and scroll wheel

Now continue with the other mouse parts:

↕️

Scroll-wheel check

Scroll di wheel up and down. The up/down arrows suppose flash, and both scroll counters suppose increase.

↔️

Side-side check

Press the two side sides, usually B4 and B5. The matching area suppose light up and the correct count suppose rise.

Note:Some side sides fit need official control app before full help go show, but basic side answer still suppose show here.

4

Check double-tap wahala

Double-tap spotting na one important part of dis page. E helps you know whether mouse micro-switch don begin wear out:

⚡

Normal double-tap

Tap di left mouse side two times at normal speed, around 200-500ms apart. If everything dey okay, the side area go show green highlight and the log go record normal double tap.

⚠️

Faulty double-tap wahala sign

If one single press dey often register like two extremely fast taps, the page go mark am as faulty double-tap wahala. Red highlight and log entry go help you notice am.

Why dis matter:Frequent faulty double-tap wahala usually means the micro-switch don age and fit soon need cleaning, repair, or replacement.
5

How to read result

After testing, use the right-side panel to judge the mouse condition well:

✅

Normal look

All sides show matching "Press" and "Release" counts, the wheel answers in both directions, and double-tap result stays inside normal range.

❌

Abnormal look

Some side count no rise, press/release counts no match, wheel fails in one direction, or faulty double-tap wahala keeps showing too often.

Event record:Di event record for the lower-right side keeps time and details of every action, so you fit review wetin happen during the check.

Reset function:Tap the "Reset All Data" side to clear counts and logs if you want start fresh again.

Common wahala and how to fix am

Wahala: Sides no dey answer

Wet fit cause am:1) Mouse no connect well; 2) Test area never activate; 3) Physical side don spoil.

Wetin you fit do:Check USB connection, make sure mouse enter the test area, then try the side for another app too.

Wahala: Scroll wheel no dey answer

Wet fit cause am:1) Wheel encoder don wear; 2) Your page no handle wheel event well; 3) System mouse settings get wahala.

Wetin you fit do:Try scroll for other pages, check whether your page supports wheel events, and adjust your system mouse wheel settings.

Wahala: Faulty double-tap wahala dey happen too often

Wet fit cause am:Mouse micro-switch don age or contact don bad. Na common hardware wahala be dis.

Wetin you fit do:Clean the micro-switch area, adjust double-tap speed setting, or replace the mouse if the wahala don too much.

Wahala: Side sides no dey show

Wet fit cause am:1) Mouse control app fit still need install; 2) Page no support side-side events well; 3) Side-side function don disable.

Wetin you fit do:Install official control app, try another page, and check side-side settings inside the mouse app.
