Question people dey ask well well

How you fit catch mouse double-tap fault?

Tap each mouse side normally 50 to 100 times while you dey watch di counters. If di faulty double-tap count dey rise when you just tap once, hardware wahala dey likely. Di page marks very short repeated taps below di line as faulty double tap.

Wetin be difference between normal double tap and faulty one?

Normal double tap na two taps wey you intentionally do within around 80 to 500ms. Faulty double tap happen when one physical press register like two taps because switch bounce or wear show up, often below 80ms.

How you fit adjust detection sensitivity?

Use di threshold slider under di mouse picture. Lower values like 50 to 70ms make check stricter. Higher values like 100 to 150ms make am softer. Default 80ms works for most normal mice.

Why my mouse dey show double-tap wahala?

Double-tap faults na usually caused by:

  • Mechanical switch wear from plenty use
  • Dust wey gather inside di switch
  • Oxidation for switch contact points
  • Factory defect for di micro-switch

Which mouse brands dey show dis wahala pass?

Any mechanical-switch mouse fit get dis problem, but people often report am for:

  • Logitech gaming mice
  • Razer optical and mechanical-switch models
  • SteelSeries gaming mice
  • Any mouse wey uses Omron or similar mechanical switch

Most times, na because of di specific switch model dem use, no be simply because di whole brand bad.

How you go know say mouse need replacement?

Think about replacement if:

  • 1) Faulty double-tap count dey stay high for main sides;
  • 2) Sides no dey answer well or need too much force;
  • 3) Wahala still dey after cleaning and basic troubleshooting;
  • 4) Di wahala don begin affect work or gaming play.

Dis page helps you judge di matter better because e puts real numbers on di wahala.

How dis double-tap detection take work technically?

Di page uses JavaScript to track mouse down and up events with millisecond timing. When one side press happen, e checks di time from di last press of dat same side. If di gap too short, e counts am as faulty double tap. If di gap normal, e counts am as standard double tap.
