Mouse check page walk-through

Step-by-step way to test mouse sides, scroll wheel, side sides, and common fault signs wey fit show.

📖

About dis tool

Dis mouse check page helps you quickly know whether all main mouse functions still dey work well, without installing any app. Di page gives live answer and data feedback so you fit understand your mouse condition better.

E supports left side, right side, middle side, two side sides (B4/B5), scroll wheel up/down, and double-tap wahala spotting.

1

Enter di test area

Move your mouse into di "mouse check area". Once mouse enter, di border go turn blue and status go show say testing don activate.

Important note:All testing functions only work inside di test area. Make sure your mouse really dey inside di gray bordered box.
🖱️

Check-area status signs

Border color dey show current test status:

  • Gray: Dey ready
  • Blue: Don active
  • Light Blue: Testing dey happen
  • Green: Double-tap don show
  • Red: Fault don show
2

Check mouse sides

Inside di test area, tap each mouse side one by one:

🖱️

Left-side check

Tap di left mouse side and watch whether di left-side area lights up. Di count for left side suppose increase for di right panel.

🖱️

Right-side check

Tap di right mouse side and confirm say di right-side area lights up. Di right-side count suppose also increase.

🖱️

Middle-side check

Press di mouse wheel as middle side. Di middle area suppose light up and middle-side count suppose rise.

Key thing to watch:Each side suppose give clear press and release feedback. For normal side, di press and release counts suppose match.
3

Check side sides and scroll wheel

Now continue with di other mouse functions:

↕️

Scroll-wheel check

Scroll di wheel up and down. Di up and down arrows suppose flash, and both counters for scroll up and scroll down suppose increase.

↔️

Side-side check

Press di two side sides, usually B4 and B5. Di matching area suppose light up and di correct counter suppose rise.

Note:Some side sides fit depend on control app for full function, but basic side answer should still show here.

4

Check double-tap fault

Double-tap detection na one important part of dis page. E helps you know whether mouse micro-switch don begin age or bounce badly:

⚡

Normal double tap

Double-tap di left side at normal speed, around 200 to 500ms apart. If all dey okay, di area go show green and di log go record normal double-tap.

⚠️

Faulty double tap

If one single press dey often register like two very fast taps, di page go mark am as faulty double tap. Red highlight and log entry go help you notice am.

Why dis matter:Frequent faulty double-tap usually means di micro-switch don wear out and fit soon need cleaning, repair, or replacement.
5

How to read result

After you finish testing, use di right-side data panel to judge mouse condition:

✅

Normal look

All sides show matching press and release counts, scroll wheel responds both ways, and double-tap detection stays inside normal range.

❌

Abnormal look

Side count no rise, press and release counts no match, one scroll direction no answer, or faulty double-tap count dey show too often.

Event record:Di record for bottom right keeps time and details of every action, so you fit review wetin happen during testing.

Reset function:Tap di "Reset everything Data" button to clear all counts and logs if you want start afresh.

Common wahala and how to fix am

Wahala: Sides no dey answer

Wet fit cause am:1) Mouse never connect well; 2) Test area never activate; 3) Physical side don spoil.

Wetin you fit do:Check USB connection, make sure mouse enter di test area, then test di same side for another app.

Wahala: Scroll wheel no dey answer

Wet fit cause am:1) Wheel encoder don wear; 2) the page no handle wheel events well; 3) system mouse setting get issue.

Wetin you fit do:Try scroll for other pages, check whether the page app supports wheel events, and adjust your system mouse wheel settings.

Wahala: Faulty double-tap dey happen too often

Wet fit cause am:Mouse micro-switch don old or contact don bad. Na common mouse hardware wahala be dis.

Wetin you fit do:Clean di micro-switch area, adjust double-tap speed settings, or replace di mouse if e don too bad.

Wahala: Side sides no dey show

Wet fit cause am:1) Mouse driver fit still need install; 2) the page app no support side-side events well; 3) side-side function don disable.

Wetin you fit do:Install official mouse control app, try another page, and check side-side settings inside the mouse app.
