What na CPS?

CPS, wey mean taps per second, na the number of mouse taps person fit do inside one second. E helps show finger reaction speed and how fast person fit tap. Most people often dey around 4 to 8 CPS, while game people fit reach 10 to 15 or more.

How person fit improve CPS score?

To improve CPS, person need practice and better method: 1) keep wrist relaxed; 2) use the right finger movement; 3) practice steady rhythm; 4) use mouse wey suit you; 5) train often so muscle memory fit stay strong. E good make person start with 5-second mode before moving higher.

Why test result fit change?

Tap-speed result fit change because of different things: 1) how tired your fingers don be; 2) room temperature; 3) the surface under the mouse; 4) your mind state; 5) the time you run the check. The best way na to run am more than once under the same condition and use the mean result.

What na di differences between test modes?

Different check modes dey check different strengths:

  • 1-second burst test: checks your highest sharp-sharp tap speed
  • 5-second precision test: checks short-time steady tapping
  • 10-second standard check: gives fuller picture of your tap strength
  • 60-second endurance test: checks how long you fit keep tapping well

Why check different mouse sides?

Checking different sides helps you understand your full mouse control:

  • Left-side check: normal tapping strength for most tasks
  • Right-side check: control and flexibility for tasks and some games
  • Middle-side check: middle finger strength and precision
  • Any mode: overall hand coordination

How to evaluate my CPS level?

Based on common run patterns, CPS levels fit look like this:

  • Average: 4-8 CPS
