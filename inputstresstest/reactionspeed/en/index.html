What is Reaction Speed Test?

Reaction speed test is a tool that measures the time required for a person to respond to visual stimuli. When you see the screen color change (from white to blue), you need to click as quickly as possible. The system precisely measures the time interval between the appearance of the stimulus and your click action.

Why is Reaction Speed Important?

Reaction speed is an important indicator of brain processing speed and hand-eye coordination. Good reaction speed is crucial in many areas:

  • Sports: Athletes need quick reactions to respond to changes in competition
  • Driving Safety: Quick reaction ability can help avoid traffic accidents
  • E-sports: Professional gamers usually have exceptional reaction speeds
  • Daily Life: Fast reactions can help deal with unexpected situations

How to Improve Reaction Speed?

Through regular training, you can improve your reaction speed:

  • Regular Practice: Use this tool to test several times daily
  • Stay Focused: Concentrate during testing, reduce distractions
  • Healthy Diet: Balanced nutrition helps brain function
  • Adequate Sleep: Good sleep optimizes cognitive performance
  • Physical Exercise: Appropriate exercise improves overall reaction ability

Normal Reaction Time Range

The average person's reaction time is typically between 200-300 milliseconds. Trained individuals can achieve below 150 milliseconds, while professional athletes and e-sports players can even go below 100 milliseconds. Age, fatigue level, and attention level all affect reaction time.
